Ranching Tip

Body Condition Scoring — Why It Matters Pre-Calving

December 2024  ·  MYT Enterprises Ranch Team

Body condition scoring (BCS) is one of the most valuable management tools a cow-calf producer can use, especially in the weeks leading up to calving. Cows at a BCS of 5–6 going into calving tend to have shorter intervals to rebreeding, better colostrum quality, and higher calf survival rates.

We evaluate BCS on all our cows in late fall and adjust feeding accordingly. It takes time, but the payoff at calving time is well worth it.
