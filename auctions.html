Winter Online Auction — Final Results

January 2025  ·  MYT Enterprises Auction Team

Our winter online auction wrapped up with strong bidder turnout. Over 80 lots were sold, with several pieces of equipment exceeding estimate. Thank you to everyone who participated as buyers and consignors.
